Restore Functionality

The restore feature allows you to recover songs you've accidentally deleted. When you delete a song, it's moved to a "Recently Deleted" section where it stays for 30 days before being permanently removed.

You can restore individual songs or restore all deleted songs at once. This prevents accidental data loss and gives you peace of mind when managing your playlist.
